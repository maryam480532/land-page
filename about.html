About Our School

Government High School was established in 1985 with the aim of providing quality and affordable education to students from all sections of society. The school is committed to academic excellence, discipline, and the overall development of every child.

Our dedicated teachers use modern teaching methods to create an engaging learning environment. Along with academics, equal importance is given to sports, cultural activities, and moral education to shape responsible citizens.
